Ghostwriter

1. The Essence

If you had to describe your vibe in just three words, what would they be? Not what you aspire to be — what your people would actually say.

2. The Geek-Out

What are three niche topics or hobbies you could talk about for hours? The more specific the better — not "music," try "90s Memphis rap."

3. The Micro-Story

Describe a perfect moment or your "happy place" using sensory details. What does it smell like? Sound like? Feel like?

4. The Typical Sunday

If money and work were irrelevant, how would you spend a random Sunday from morning to night? Be honest, not aspirational.

5. The Roast Story

What's a specific, funny story your friends tell to roast you? The one that comes up at every dinner. Self-awareness is attractive.

6. The Younger Self

What would your younger self be most surprised — or proud — to see in your life today? Even small things reveal a lot.

7. The Value Proposition

What's the specific "win" someone gets by dating you? Not personality traits — concrete things. "I always find the best restaurant nobody's heard of yet."

8. The Vision

What is your honest vision for a relationship in the next 3–5 years? Clarity is a green flag. Be direct.

9. The Ideal Partner

Describe three qualities in a partner that matter more to you than height, job, or any other "stat." Go deeper than "kind" or "funny."

10. The Deal-Breaker

What's your one non-negotiable — framed as a hope for your future, not a warning. "I need someone who wants a real family someday" lands better than "don't message me if you're not serious."
